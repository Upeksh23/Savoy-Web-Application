About Us

Welcome to Savoy Cinema, Colombo, Sri Lanka's top movie theatre for movie buffs. Ever since our founding, our mission has been to give our customers the best possible movie experience. Savoy Cinema is the ideal location for spending quality time with loved ones while watching a movie, thanks to its cutting-edge amenities and extensive lineup of the newest blockbuster movies.


Our Facilities


  • Modern Auditoriums: Equipped with cutting-edge sound and projection technology to offer an immersive viewing experience.

  • Comfortable Seating: Enjoy our plush seating designed for maximum comfort, ensuring a relaxing and enjoyable time.

  • Concession Stand: A variety of snacks and beverages are available to enhance your movie experience.

  • Online Booking: Convenient online ticket booking system, allowing you to reserve your seats in advance.

Our Commitment

At Savoy Cinema, we are committed to delivering the best in entertainment and customer service. Our friendly staff is always ready to assist you and ensure your visit is memorable.


Special Events and Promotions

We regularly host special screenings, events, and promotional offers. Stay tuned to our website and social media channels for the latest updates.
